Regular Pizza

A regular pizza typically consists of a circular-shaped base made from dough, which is topped with tomato sauce and cheese, often mozzarella. Additional toppings such as pepperoni, mushrooms, onions, peppers, olives, sausage, and various meats or vegetables can be added according to personal preference. The pizza is then baked in an oven until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
